Welcome to Transient.chat

A real-time, temporary chatroom where conversations don’t stick around.

Messages vanish automatically after 1 hour... nothing is saved.

This isn’t a social network. No profiles... no feeds... no algorithms.
Just people chatting live... like the early days of the internet.

Also join a Watchroom to experience synchronized YouTube viewing with your chatmates.

Transient.chat runs completely in memory.
No disk writes... no databases... no stored content.
When a message expires, it’s gone.
When a room is empty, everything disappears with it.
If you don’t return, your handle gets released to someone else.

Nothing is archived. Nothing is retained. Everything is ephemeral.

If you miss casual conversation without permanence... you’re in the right place.

Come on in and chat—or watch and chat.
