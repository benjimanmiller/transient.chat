Welcome to Transient.chat

A live, text-only chat room for conversations that don’t stick around.

Messages disappear after 1 hour.
Usernames are first-come, first-served — keep chatting every few days and it stays yours.

This isn’t a social network. There are no profiles, no feeds, no algorithms.
Just people talking in real time, like the early internet.

Transient.chat runs entirely in memory — no database, no disk, no logs.
When messages expire, they’re gone. When the server restarts, everything resets.
If you don't come back your handle gets released.

Nothing is archived. Nothing is tracked. Everything is ephemeral.

If you miss casual conversation without permanence, screenshots, or pressure — you’re in the right place.

Come say something. It won’t be here forever.
